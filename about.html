About me

Thanks for checking out my About Me section!

I’m a Technical Program Manager (TPM) currently based out of the Pacific Northwest where I live with my wife, son and daughter. In my free time I enjoy spending time with my family going for walks and whenever possible traveling to new destinations and exploring countries and areas we haven’t been to before.

I am passionate about mobile tech and an avid reader of all the top tech blogs and news sites. I also enjoy playing around with mobile development platforms (mostly iOS) exploring new features and gaining a deeper understanding by building “hello world” apps and trying them out.

I enjoy working on projects that directly impact end-users, especially UI/UX related projects. Getting the satisfaction of seeing something that I directly worked on being used by millions of people is something that keeps me motivated every day.

I am also passionate about improving collaboration between cross functional teams. As a TPM I work very closely with engineering and product teams and ensuring that teams are aligned on priorities and communicate efficiently. I am always looking for new ideas to try out with our team and iterate on.
